Residential

Homes built and renovated with care.

Each project is individual, so we combine our expertise with our design team to create spaces that suit your needs. We respect your home and aim to complete your project to the best standard and on time, with minimal disruption to you and your family.

We take pride in our renovation coverage and quality. We understand that renovating can be stressful and disruptive, and we work to make it easier. Our residential portfolio covers a broad scope of projects.

Your home is a significant investment, so when you are planning alterations it pays to discuss your needs with the team at Thomsen. We focus on quality work, finished properly from start to completion.

We build self-contained living areas, usually on the grounds of a single-family home. A granny flat can be standalone or attached to the existing dwelling, and is built to the same standard as the rest of your home.

Commercial

Maintenance, fit-outs and repairs.

Our commercial maintenance covers properties that are not for domestic use, such as offices, retail stores and the communal areas in apartment blocks. If you own property, we look after the general condition of your premises, both inside and out.

We make interior spaces suitable for occupation. This is often used for office developments, where the base construction is completed by the developer and the final fit-out is completed for the occupant, who is generally leasing the space as a tenant.

Our tradespeople and office staff place a high value on customer service, including on insurance repairs. We can typically schedule your appointment within one to three days and plan appointment times that work for you.

We specialise in remedial and rectification works across building and insurance jobs. By focusing on this work, we keep projects on time with a quality finish. Our staff and contractors are supported with training from Thomsen and from third party safety and compliance regulators, so the standard of work stays high.
